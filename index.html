Crystal Collecting

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each crystal is worth a different amount, but you will not know what until you click on it!

Click on the crystals to add to your score to match the goal.

Careful though! If you go over, you will lose the game.

Each time the game starts, the crystals will be set to different values.
